About SharingInc. and TheBlogChain

Since the inception of our services, we have always performed & attempted, to the best of our abilities, to deliver our clients with exceptional services at competitive rates.

Regardless of whether you are looking for a professional finishing touch to your website, or looking to build one from scratch; whether you are in need of a graphic designer to visualise your projects, or content writers to describe your ideas eloquently;

whether you desire a data analyst to crunch through those cumbersome numbers to provide you useful insights from the data; or you are just searching for some innovative art pieces to showcase the grandeur of your office and/or home to your friends & colleagues — Sharing Inc. is the one stop shop for your requirements.

Who We Are.

An initiative taken up by a bunch of young individuals looking to do something productive in the information market, The BlogChain is a website, as must be clear from the name itself, for blogs. Now, you must be asking yourself (or you might not be, honestly the writer of this text couldn’t think of any other wording than this cliché as a conjunction on to the next point for some reason), blogs related to what exactly? The answer to this question cannot be limited to any one topic, just as the blogs you might be finding here in the future.

Our Mission.

News, technology, current affairs, art, literature, general knowledge, sports, anything we fancy we know a satisfactorily good deal about, will be penned down here. However, primarily, as should once again be clear from the name itself, you will find all the tantalizing information, analysis, insights and developments we can provide to you, about the unimaginably vast new field the world has been tinkering with recently, the cryptocurrency space and the technology on which they work, blockchains.

Our Vision.

Our vision and plans for the future here are simple, inculcate this place to be a platform where you can get to learn, and you can get to share what you learn. The features we plan to roll out as envisaged by our team will not only enrich our, and your, experience here, but eventually, and hopefully, help us be a one-stop shop for all the knowledge of the new and exciting things happening in this world.

Our Values.

Our values are to provide our readers with the most authentic, unbiased inputs about the new developments happening in the world in a wide spectrum of fields like News, technology, current affairs, art, literature, general knowledge, sports and other fields.
